FAQ

What is InterviewWatchdog?

InterviewWatchdog is an AI-powered platform designed to detect cheating and suspicious behavior during remote or in-person interviews. It helps companies ensure a fair and transparent hiring process.

How does it detect phone usage or ChatGPT overlays?

Our system employs computer vision and screen analysis to catch movements associated with phone usage, as well as in-browser AI text overlays. Proprietary algorithms continuously scan for anomalies that signify cheating.

Which platforms does InterviewWatchdog integrate with?

We provide direct integrations with popular technical testing platforms like Hackerrank, Codility, HireVue, and many others to streamline your interview workflow.

Is it easy to set up?

Absolutely. You can start using InterviewWatchdog by embedding a simple script or linking our service to your existing interview platform. Most customers get it running in under an hour.

How does InterviewWatchdog handle privacy and data security?

We take privacy and data protection very seriously. All interview data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and we adhere to global data protection regulations such as GDPR and CCPA. We also provide customizable retention policies, allowing you to store or discard data based on your company's specific compliance needs.

Can InterviewWatchdog detect second monitors or screen sharing?

Yes. Our advanced screen-monitoring algorithms can detect typical signs of multi-monitor setups and unauthorized screen sharing. Whenever suspicious activity is identified, the system flags the event and includes additional context in the final report for further review.

What if the system generates false positives?

False positives are rare but can occur with any detection tool. If something is flagged incorrectly, our analytics dashboard allows you to review and mark events as benign. We continuously refine our algorithms based on feedback to reduce inaccuracies over time.

Is InterviewWatchdog suitable for all interview types?

Absolutely. Whether it's a technical coding assessment, a behavioral interview, or a remote screening call, InterviewWatchdog's AI can help ensure fairness by monitoring candidates in real time. Our tools are designed to adapt to both remote and in-person setups.

How do I interpret risk scores and flagged events?

Each suspicious event is assigned a risk score based on severity. The higher the risk score, the stronger the likelihood of cheating or policy violation. You can click into each flagged event for timestamps, screenshots, or activity details, making it easy to decide if further investigation is warranted.

How do candidates provide consent for monitoring?

Before starting an interview, candidates are presented with a clear disclosure statement outlining the monitoring practices and data usage. Candidates must agree to the terms before proceeding, ensuring transparency and compliance with relevant regulations.

What support channels do you offer?

We provide comprehensive support through email, live chat, and phone. Our support team is available to guide you during setup, troubleshoot technical issues, and offer best practices to maximize the effectiveness of InterviewWatchdog.
